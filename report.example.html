AI Agents Race in July 2025

Source: Medium

Date: July 21, 2025

In July 2025, significant developments are occurring in the realm of AI agentic systems. Amazon has launched a platform dedicated to building AI agents, while Google has introduced an AI capability that can make phone calls on behalf of users. This fast-paced innovation also brings about both opportunities and challenges, as stakeholders navigate the promises and misunderstandings surrounding AI systems.

AI agents are here, but companies are still learning how to put them to use

Source: Fortune Asia

Date: July 22, 2025

Vivek Luthra, Accenture's Asia-Pacific data and AI lead, highlights the current challenges faced by companies in effectively leveraging AI agents. Speaking at the Fortune Brainstorm AI conference in Singapore, Luthra notes that most businesses have adopted AI agents primarily as assistants or advisors, rather than deploying them as autonomous entities capable of independent decision-making. This indicates a significant gap between available AI technology and its practical application in business operations.

Day 29: AWS Agentic AI “NEW”s (16 July 2025)

Source: Medium

Date: July 16, 2025

AWS is enhancing its capabilities in Agentic AI, focusing on enabling new models and expanding application functionalities. Key updates include the introduction of Nova model customization within SageMaker AI, featuring turnkey recipes for pre-training, fine-tuning, and alignment on the Nova FM family. Additionally, the NOVA ACT research preview offers developers new opportunities to explore. AWS aims to provide comprehensive services to deploy and operate these advanced technologies effectively.
